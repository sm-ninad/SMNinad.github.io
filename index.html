About Me.

I am S. M. Ninad, a 3D Designer and Asset Developer passionate about bringing digital worlds to life. With expertise in Blender, I specialize in crafting 3D assets, and characters that enhance immersive experiences in games and interactive media.
Currently, I am working at Unicorns Codes Ltd., where I contribute to developing visually compelling assets that align with creative and technical requirements. My work extends beyond my role, as I actively engage in personal and collaborative projects, trying to push the boundaries of 3D artistry.
One of my notable projects is Earth SOS, which is an under development environmental awareness game where players embark on a mission to restore a polluted world. This project reflects my dedication to meaningful storytelling and impactful game design.
